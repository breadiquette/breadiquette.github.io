This! Is! Breadiquette!

Hello and welcome to Breadiquette! If you're new here, I'm Clara, and this is my blog! Breadiquette is the result of countless hours of scheming, both from myself and from a good friend Chris, a crazy smart computer scientiest to whom I probably still owe a few loaves of bread.

I wanted to create Breadiquette for a few reasons. First, as I moved a way from friends and family for school, I wanted an outlet through which to keep my loved ones updated on my baking endeavors. Additionally, I've found baking to be a simultaneously stimulating and relaxing respite from my studies. Finally, I believe good food should be shared, even if only through a computer screen. I encourage you to bake along with me, and let me know how it goes!

By way of thanks, I want to first shout out the aformentioned creator of this blog, Chris! I have immense gratitude for his invaluable support. I'd like to also thank my mom, for encouraging me to continue making sourdough even when I don't produce the results I want. I want to also mention my sister, Mary, for providing moral support, and for being my role model and best friend. This blog is a tribute to all my family and friends who have encouraged me in both my personal and my academic pursuits. Since I can't make them all bread, I can at least share with them virtually.

If you want to stay in touch, scroll down to the bottom of this page and enter your email in the box. Doing so will allow you to get notifications whenever I post, which, given my busy schedule, may be a little inconsistent at times. Or head over to the Contact button at the top of this page to send me a message directly! Hope to hear from you soon.
